Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Login" button in the top navigation bar, then select "Register" on the login page. Fill out the registration form with your details and submit.

How can I upload my notes?

After logging in, navigate to your dashboard and click on the "Upload Notes" button. Fill out the form with details about your notes and upload the file.

Are all the notes free to download?

Yes, all notes on FAST Parhai are free to download. We believe in making education accessible to everyone.

How can I report inappropriate content?

If you find any inappropriate content, click on the "Report" button available on every note or forum post. Fill out the report form with details about the issue.
